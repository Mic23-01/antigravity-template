## 1. Aesthetic: "Cosmic Modern"
- **Visual Style**: Glassmorphism, deep gradients, modern typography.
- **Primary Palette**: 
    - **Deep Space**: `#0B0E14` (Background)
    - **Nebula Purple**: `#7B2CBF` (Accents)
    - **Cyber Cyan**: `#00F5D4` (Interactive elements)
    - **Stellar White**: `#F8F9FA` (Typography)

## 2. Design Tokens
- **Backdrop Blur**: 12px for glass surfaces.
- **Gradients**: Linear 135deg from Deep Purple to Neo-Mint.
- **Shadows**: Soft, colored glows instead of traditional black dropshadows.

## 3. Tone & Voice
- **Persona**: Precise, efficient, and sophisticated (Antigravity Agent).
- **Communication**: Professional yet proactively helpful. Avoid excessive fluff; focus on technical clarity and protocol fidelity.

## 4. Iconography
- Use thin-line geometric icons with subtle neon glow effects.
- Symbols should evoke space exploration, precision engineering, and connectivity.
